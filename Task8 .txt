Insights found in the dashboard


Top-Performing Segment – The Consumer segment appears to be generating the highest sales overall, suggesting that targeted marketing campaigns towards this group could yield higher returns compared to Corporate or Home Office segments.

Category-Wise Sales Concentration – Technology products contribute significantly to total sales, especially within the Consumer segment. This indicates a strong demand for tech-related items, making it a strategic focus area for upselling and bundling.

Profitability Gaps Across Categories – While some categories (like Office Supplies) generate steady sales volume, their profit contribution is lower compared to Technology. This points to a need for pricing adjustments or cost optimizations in lower-margin categories.

Segment–Category Combinations for Growth – The Technology–Consumer and Office Supplies–Corporate combinations stand out as profitable and high-sales clusters. Expanding product variety and marketing efforts in these combinations can help maximize revenue.
